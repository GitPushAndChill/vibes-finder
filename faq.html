Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about using Vibes Finder to discover places across Dutch cities.

What is Vibes Finder?

Vibes Finder is a curated city guide that helps you discover places in the Netherlands by mood, vibe, and city.

How do I find places for a specific city or vibe?

Go to the Vibes page and use the city and vibe filters. You can combine both filters to narrow down to exactly what you want.

What does each vibe mean?

On the Vibes page, use the “Learn about the Vibes” button to open definitions for each vibe category and see what fits your mood.

Can I see details for a place before visiting?

Yes. Click a post card to open more details, including longer descriptions, images, and address information.

How does the map work?

The homepage map shows location markers for available posts. Click a marker to preview the place and open the related post.

How can I suggest a place or ask a question?

Use the Contact Us page and send a message via Instagram. Suggestions and collaborations are welcome.
