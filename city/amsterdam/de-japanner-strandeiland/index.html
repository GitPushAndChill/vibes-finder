De Japanner Strandeiland Amsterdam – Waterside Sushi for Summer Evenings

Amsterdam

Looking for a waterside restaurant in Amsterdam? De Japanner Strandeiland is worth it for sushi, cocktails, and one of the better summer terraces by the water.

De Japanner Strandeiland makes most sense on a warm day, when the terrace and water views do half the work for you. The sushi and cocktails keep things light, the setting feels more relaxed than central Amsterdam, and it is an easy choice for a summer date, a catch-up with friends, or a dinner that leans more scenic than formal. The downside is that the location is part of the appeal, so bad weather takes a lot away, and it is not the place for a cozy winter dinner or a budget meal. Skip it when the forecast is weak or you want something more intimate, and go when you want sunshine, space, and a casual evening by the water.
