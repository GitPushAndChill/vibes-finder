Brouwerij 't IJ

Amsterdam

Looking for a brewery in Amsterdam that feels actually worth the stop? Brouwerij 't IJ stands out for local beer, windmill views, and an easy tasting-room atmosphere.

Brouwerij 't IJ is one of the most reliable beer stops in Amsterdam, and not just because of the windmill next to it. The house-brewed beers are the main reason to go, the tasting setup is straightforward, and it works well for visitors who want something distinctly local without overcomplicating the plan. The downside is that it is a known spot, so it can feel touristy and busy, especially in good weather. Skip it if you want a hidden beer bar or a quieter local pub, and go when you are happy with a classic Amsterdam brewery experience.
