Kastan

Hoofddorp

Kastan is the cosiest biercafé in Hoofddorp — perfect for post-commute pints and chill hangs near the station.

Tucked right by the busy Hoofddorp station, Kastan vibes like a warm, renovated boerenschuur turned buzzy biercafé — think exposed beams, friendly crowds and a chill soundtrack that makes Thursday feel like the place-to-be. The menu leans comfy with burgers and hearty mains, plus a solid rotation of beers on tap including a 1515 on draft, making it the easygoing, beer-lovers-approved spot in Hoofddorp.

Kruisweg 666, 2132 CJ Hoofddorp, Netherlands
