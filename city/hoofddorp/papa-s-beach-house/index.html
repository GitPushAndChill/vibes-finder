Papa's Beach House

Hoofddorp

Looking for one of the best summer spots in Hoofddorp? Papa's Beach House at Haarlemmermeerse Bos is perfect for SUP rental, lakeside lounging, and relaxed outdoor days with friends.

Papa's Beach House is one of those easy wins for a warm day in the Netherlands: rent a SUP, head onto the water, then settle in by the lake for drinks and a laid-back afternoon. The setting inside Haarlemmermeerse Bos gives it a true outdoorsy feel, with enough space to switch between activity and downtime without ever feeling rushed. If you are building a list of the best places in the Netherlands for golden-hour hangouts, this Hoofddorp favorite deserves a spot. It works for casual group plans, spontaneous weekend outings, and anyone who wants a simple mix of nature, water, and summer energy.

Location

IJweg 961, 2131 LV Hoofddorp, Netherlands
