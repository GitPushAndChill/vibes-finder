Privacy Policy

How Vibes Finder handles data, consent, analytics, and ads.

1. Who we are

Vibes Finder is a content website that helps users discover places by city and vibe in the Netherlands.

2. What data we process

We may process technical information such as browser details, pages visited, and approximate location data through analytics and ad technologies when you provide consent.

3. Legal basis

For optional analytics and advertising processing, we rely on your consent. You can reject or withdraw consent at any time through the Privacy settings control.

4. Advertising and analytics providers

We use Google services including Google Analytics and Google AdSense. These services may set cookies and process data according to your consent choices.

5. Your rights

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have rights to access, rectify, erase, and restrict processing of personal data, and to withdraw consent.

6. Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent via the contact options listed on the Contact page.
